About Salon Management System

The Salon Management System is designed to automate and simplify daily salon operations. It provides an easy way to manage employees, customers, services, branches and appointments from one centralized system.


The dashboard gives real-time information about the salon's business activities and helps managers make better decisions using visual reports and statistics.


Main Features:

  • Employee Management
  • Branch Management
  • Service Management
  • Customer Management
  • Appointment Scheduling
  • Business Analytics Dashboard
